Tuna Jell-O Pie
Voilà, the “Summer Salad Pie” – basically this pie is made up of a lemon jello layer with tomato sauce, celery, olives and onion, in a cheese crust topped with tuna salad. Yes, tuna salad. "turkeysandwich.wordpress.com"
Ingredients
Summer Salad Pie
  • 3 oz. lemon-flavored Jell-O
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of boiling water
  • 8 oz. of tomato soup
  • 1 tbsp. vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • A few drops of Worcestershire Sauce and Tabasco
  • A dash of pepper
  • 1/2 cup each chopped celery and pimiento-stuffed olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
Cheese Pie Shell
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 cup plus 1 tbsp. of shortening
  • 1/2 shredded American cheese
  • 2 tbsp. water
Tuna Salad
  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 1 tsp. of lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. of minced onion
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Mayonnaise or salad dressing
Directions
I.
  • Heat oven to 475 degrees Freedom units.
  • Mix flour and salt, then cut the shortening thoroughly.
  • Stir in the cheese while sprinkling water gradually over the mixture, 1 tbsp at a time, tossing lightly with a fork each addition.
  • Gather dough into ball, then roll out 1" larger than inverted 9" pie pan on a lightly floured cloth-covered board.
  • Ease into pan; flute and prick pastry. Bake to 8 to 10 mins.; cool.
    • II.
  • Lightly mix tuna, lemon juice, onion, and celery.
  • Season with salt and paprika to taste. Chill.
  • Just before serving, drain and mix in just enough mayonnaise to moisten.
    • III.
  • Dissolve the Jell-O in boiling water and stir in tomato sauce, vinegar, and seasonings.
  • Chill until slightly thickened.
  • Fold in celery, olives, and onions
  • Pour into cooled Cheese Pie Shell.
  • Chill thoroughly, then spoon tuna salad on top of pie. 6 servings.

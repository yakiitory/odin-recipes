Tuna Jell-O Pie

The Perfect Summer Treat!

Ingredients:

Summer Salad Pie

  • 3 oz. lemon-flavored Jell-O
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of boiling water
  • 8 oz. of tomato soup
  • 1 tbsp. vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • A few drops of Worcestershire Sauce and Tabasco
  • A dash of pepper
  • 1/2 cup each chopped celery and pimiento-stuffed olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion

Cheese Pie Shell

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 cup plus 1 tbsp. of shortening
  • 1/2 shredded American cheese
  • 2 tbsp. water

Tuna Salad

  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 1 tsp. of lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. of minced onion
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Mayonnaise or salad dressing

Directions:

I.

  1. Heat oven to 475 degrees Freedom units.
  2. Mix flour and salt, then cut the shortening thoroughly.
  3. Stir in the cheese while sprinkling water gradually over the mixture, 1 tbsp at a time, tossing lightly with a fork each addition.
  4. Gather dough into ball, then roll out 1" larger than inverted 9" pie pan on a lightly floured cloth-covered board.
  5. Ease into pan; flute and prick pastry. Bake to 8 to 10 mins.; cool.

II.

  1. Lightly mix tuna, lemon juice, onion, and celery.
  2. Season with salt and paprika to taste. Chill.
  3. Just before serving, drain and mix in just enough mayonnaise to moisten.

III.

  1. Dissolve the Jell-O in boiling water and stir in tomato sauce, vinegar, and seasonings.
  2. Chill until slightly thickened.
  3. Fold in celery, olives, and onions
  4. Pour into cooled Cheese Pie Shell.
  5. Chill thoroughly, then spoon tuna salad on top of pie. 6 servings.
